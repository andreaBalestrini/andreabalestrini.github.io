Photography

In the last years I started to look at the world of photography. I like to look through my photos to show the world as I see it, focusing, in particular, on the nature that surrounds us.
In my shots I use the old polaroid format (1:1) and I don't use photo editing programs.
I'm not a photographer, I'm just a person who likes to photograph.
